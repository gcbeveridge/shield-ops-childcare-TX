# Phase 2A+ Design Enhancement: Guardian Warmth Aesthetic

## Context
Phase 2A created the priority heat map functionality. Now we're enhancing it with a distinctive, welcoming design that matches the Shield Ops brand identity.

## Design Philosophy
**"Supported & Empowered"** - Users should feel:
- ✅ Welcome, not overwhelmed
- ✅ Clear on what to do
- ✅ Supported by thoughtful design
- ✅ Confident, not stressed
- ✅ Like the app was designed specifically for them

## Tasks
