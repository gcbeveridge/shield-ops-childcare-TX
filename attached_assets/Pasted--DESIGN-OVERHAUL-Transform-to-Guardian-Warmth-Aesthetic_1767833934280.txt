# DESIGN OVERHAUL: Transform to Guardian Warmth Aesthetic

## Problem
Current dashboard has generic "AI slop" aesthetic with purple gradients and corporate feel. Need complete visual transformation to match Shield Ops brand identity: warm, welcoming, supportive.

## Design Goals
Users should feel:
- ✅ "Someone really thought about me when designing this"
- ✅ Welcomed and supported, not overwhelmed
- ✅ Clear on what to do next
- ✅ Confident and empowered
- ✅ Like this was built specifically for childcare directors

## CRITICAL: Complete Visual Transformation
